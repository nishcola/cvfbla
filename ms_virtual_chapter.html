Virtual Chapter

Cumberland Valley FBLA does not currently have a chapter for Mountain View Middle School or Eagle View Middle School. However, middle schoolers are still able to join thanks to the generous provision of the FBLA Virtual Chapter from the national organization. To learn more and fill out an application, click here. Please fill out the application with a parent or guardian that can help guide you through the process.
