We are the Future Business Leaders of America.

Welcome to Cumberland Valley FBLA's website! Here, you'll find resources you need and more information about our school's chapter. FBLA is national organization in which high school students gain experience through educational programs, leadership development, and academic competition. In FBLA, you can find an event for nearly every career field, even if you do not want to go into business. With over 60+ events, you are sure to find something that you or your friends can explore! Our chapter is part of Region 26 of Pennsylvania FBLA, and one of the largest chapters in the state. We have consistently gone to the National Leadership Conference, with the 2024 conference being hosted in Orlando, FL. We have had 7 placements at the National level in recent memory, and one National champion! Be sure to practice and place well in your events, as the 2025 National Leadership Conference is being hosted in Los Angeles & Anaheim, California.

Cumberland Valley FBLA is always seeking more membership, so don't feel hesitant to tell your peers about our chapter! Feel free to explore the rest of the website if you want to learn more.
